RETURNS INFO

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery, in accordance with EU consumer law.

Returned items must be:

  • Unworn, unused, and unwashed
  • In original condition, with all tags and packaging intact

Due to the limited and artisanal nature of our pieces, we kindly ask that items are handled with care.

Exceptions
Items that are made-to-order, customized, or explicitly marked as non-returnable are not eligible for return.

Return Process
To initiate a return, please contact us at office@armorygrayscvle.com with your order number.

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the item is faulty or incorrect.

Once your return is received and inspected, a refund will be issued to the original payment method within a reasonable timeframe.

Original shipping fees are non-refundable.

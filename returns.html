Returns & Exchanges

Each Armory Grayscvle piece is created in limited quantities, with intention and care. For this reason, we do not operate on a traditional returns model.

All purchases are considered final at the time of sale.

We encourage thoughtful selection before purchasing — from sizing to silhouette — and we are always available to assist you prior to checkout with any questions you may have.

That said, we understand that issues can occur. If your item arrives with a defect, or if you receive an incorrect piece, please contact us and we will take care of the situation with attention and respect.

In certain cases, size exchanges may be considered, subject to availability.

Armory Grayscvle exists to promote conscious ownership — fewer pieces, chosen deliberately, meant to be kept.

For any concerns, please reach out to us at office@armorygrayscvle.com. We are here.
